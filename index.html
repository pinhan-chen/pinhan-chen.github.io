Hi! I am Peter, welcome to my website. I am currently pursuing a PhD in ECE at USC, working on the intersection of Machine Learning and Electronic Design Automation (EDA) for 2.5D/3D ICs Design. My research focuses on developing ML-driven solutions for 2.5D/3D IC design, including reinforcement learning for optimization and physics-driven neural networks for modeling.

Previously, I was a Research Scientist/Machine Learning Engineer at MediaTek, where piqued my interests in applying machine learning algorithms for chip design problems (Ex: macro placement). I have also developed multi-agent AI systems for RF/Analog design and built physics-aware deep learning models for advanced packaging.

Outside of research, I am also interested in leadership program (Ex: Blue Gear Program), board games (Ex: Teamfight Tactics (TFT), Othello, and pokers), and start-ups. Feel free to reach out to me for any discussions.
